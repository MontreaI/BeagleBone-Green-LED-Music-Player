Features Implemented:
	Pressing IN = play/pause
	Pressing LEFT 	= Replay (If playing less than 2 seconds)
					= Play Previous Song (If playing >= 2 seconds)
	Pressing RIGHT 	= Play Next Song
	Adjusting Potentiometer = volume up/down

Testing Features:
	Joystick up will toggle repeat
	Joystick down will toggle shuffle
	Or change the bool values of shuffle and repeat in song_data.c
